Follow the following guidelines to complete this process successfully.

  • Download the excel template and populate the data.
  • Save the excel stylesheet as *.xlsx or *.xls extension
  • Do not alter the columns in the excel template.
  • The columns in the excel file are:
  • DebitAccount - Account to be debited in VCB.
  • Amount - Transaction Amount.
  • RecipientAccount - Beneficiary's account number or phone number.
  • Beneficiary Bank - Bank the funds are being sent to (Select from a drop down provided).
  • Narration - Transaction Narration (Limit it to at most 50 characters).
  • The total number of columns in the execl fileshould be 6.
  • Use this excel template to fill your data for bulk processing.
  • All fields are Mandatory.
